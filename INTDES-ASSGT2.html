About the Singapore Zoo

The Singapore Zoo is renowned for its 'open concept,' which offers visitors the opportunity to experience and be inspired by the wonders of nature. With over 26 hectares of lush greenery, it is home to more than 2,800 animals from over 300 species. The zoo's world-class exhibits provide an immersive experience, allowing visitors to observe animals in habitats that closely mimic their natural environments.

Visitors can enjoy interactive sessions, educational talks, and fun activities designed to foster a deeper appreciation for wildlife conservation. Whether it's watching the playful antics of primates, the majestic stride of big cats, or the vibrant display of exotic birds, the Singapore Zoo offers a unique and memorable adventure for all ages.
